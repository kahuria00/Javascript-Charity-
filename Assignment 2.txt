Declaration is the means of creating a variable,when declaring a variable var is the keyword.

For example : if one wishes to declare a variable denoting cars: i.e. var cars

While Initialization is the means of assigning an initial value to a variable. 
Hence undefined is the initial value of a declared variable.
To assign a value to the variable,
 we use the equal sign i.e.  var cars =

definition is the means of assigning what value a declared variable holds
for example :var cars = ["mercedes","porche","jeep"]
